Anonymous Twilio is an anonymous text messaging client developed with two others at Boilermake 2014. Users can simply text our Twilio number the number and message to anonymously send, and our software will relay it to the desired receiver. It was developed with Ruby, Sinatra, and MongoDB. I soldered together a custom printed circuit board over the semester in my electrical engineering course. For the final project, my partner and I programmed our boards into virtual Etch-a-Sketch games. Potentiometers and push buttons on the boards served as the controls, and one board could control the other board's game using wireless communication.
