Bit-zee Bot
I built and programmed a two-wheel robot using an Arduino, motor controller, hair dryer motors, recycled camera, and sound module. The robot can move in all directions, take pictures, and record and playback sound. See it in action here.
Anonymous Twilio
Anonymous Twilio is an anonymous text messaging client developed with two others at Boilermake 2014. Users can simply text our Twilio number the number and message to anonymously send, and our software will relay it to the desired receiver. It was developed with Ruby, Sinatra, and MongoDB.
Etch-a-Sketch
I soldered together a custom printed circuit board over the semester in my electrical engineering course. For the final project, my partner and I programmed our boards into virtual Etch-a-Sketch games. Potentiometers and push buttons on the boards served as the controls, and one board could control the other board's game using wireless communication. See them in action here.
